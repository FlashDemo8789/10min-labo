Our Ingredients

Sourced from the Extraordinary

Each ingredient is selected for its exceptional properties and rarity. We spare no expense in our pursuit of perfection.

The Rarest Botanicals on Earth

From snow lotus harvested at 4,000 meters in the Japanese Alps to marine collagen from deep-sea pearls, each ingredient tells a story of rarity, purity, and extraordinary efficacy.

Our master formulators travel the world, forging relationships with specialized harvesters and growers who share our commitment to sustainability and quality.

Signature Ingredients

Nature's Most Precious Gifts

雪

Snow Lotus Extract

Japanese Alps, 4,000m Elevation

Harvested once annually during a three-week window, snow lotus thrives in extreme conditions where few plants can survive. Its cellular protection mechanisms translate to extraordinary anti-aging benefits.

Antioxidant Cell Protection Anti-Aging
金

24K Gold Flakes

Kyoto, Traditional Gold Leaf Artisans

Pure Kyoto gold, hammered to impossibly thin flakes by master artisans using 400-year-old techniques. Gold's unique properties enhance cellular renewal and improve absorption of other active ingredients.

Cellular Renewal Radiance Luxury
珠

Deep-Sea Pearl Essence

Mikimoto Waters, Ise-Shima

Akoya pearls from depths of 100 meters, harvested sustainably from pristine waters. Rich in amino acids and minerals, pearl essence has been prized in Asian beauty rituals for millennia.

Brightening Hydration Mineral-Rich
竹

Ancient Bamboo Sap

Arashiyama Sacred Groves

Extracted from 100-year-old bamboo in protected groves near Kyoto. The sap contains unique silica compounds and growth factors that promote skin regeneration and elasticity.

Regeneration Elasticity Silica-Rich
酒

Premium Sake Ferment

Niigata Brewery, 300-Year Heritage

From one of Japan's most prestigious sake breweries, this fermented rice extract contains kojic acid and enzymes that gently brighten and refine skin texture.

Brightening Exfoliation Texture
茶

First Harvest Matcha

Uji, Kyoto Prefecture

From shade-grown tea plants harvested during the first spring flush. This ceremonial-grade matcha contains the highest concentration of EGCG and L-theanine for powerful antioxidant protection.

Antioxidant Anti-Pollution Calming
