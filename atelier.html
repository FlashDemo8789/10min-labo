Private Atelier Consultations

We invite a select few to experience our private consultations at the Tokyo atelier. During your visit, you will:

  • Meet with a master formulator to discuss your skincare goals
  • Receive a comprehensive skin analysis using advanced diagnostics
  • Tour our laboratory and witness the formulation process
  • Experience personalized product recommendations
