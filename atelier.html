Our Technology

Core Innovations

Exosome Extraction

Using advanced filtration and low-temperature techniques, we isolate high-concentration exosomes from human stem cell culture liquid, preserving their regenerative signals for rapid renewal.

TDDS Engineering

Our researchers design Transdermal Drug Delivery System pathways that work in harmony with Innovative 3D Nanotechnology®, guiding actives deep beneath the stratum corneum without relying on surfactants.

Partnership with Pro Labo Holdings

Collaborating with pioneers of cell-penetrating water allows us to maintain a rich, luxurious texture that remains baby-safe while dramatically improving ingredient bioavailability.
