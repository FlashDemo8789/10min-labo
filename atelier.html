Our Technology

Core Technologies

Exosome Extraction Technology

We utilize advanced extraction methods to isolate high-concentration exosomes from human stem cell culture liquid. These nano-vesicles (30-150nm) contain growth factors, proteins, and genetic material that trigger regenerative processes when delivered to skin cells.

TDDS Development

Our research in TDDS (Transdermal Drug Delivery System) technology enables efficient penetration through the skin's stratum corneum. Combined with patented Innovative 3D Nanotechnology®, this dual-system approach delivers exosomes to deep skin layers without surfactants.

Pro Labo Holdings Partnership

Our collaboration with Pro Labo Holdings brings their innovative cell-penetrating water technology—originally developed for NK cell and stem cell culture—to cosmetic applications. This specialized water base enhances bioavailability and cellular uptake of exosomes for maximum efficacy.
