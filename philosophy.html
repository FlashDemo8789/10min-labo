Our Story

Born from Research, Driven by Family

10min labo was founded on a simple belief: skincare should be safe enough for babies yet powerful enough to deliver professional results. This seemingly impossible challenge drove us to years of extensive research and development.

Through collaboration with leading biotechnology institutes, we developed the world's first Innovative 3D Nanotechnology®—a patented delivery system that enables rapid skin penetration and visible results in just 10 minutes through simple massage application.

Today, 10min labo stands as proof that we don't have to choose between safety and efficacy. Our award-winning formulations prioritize family wellness over profit margins, creating truly good products the entire family can trust.
