Our Philosophy

間 — The Art of Space

In Japanese aesthetics, 'Ma' represents the pause, the space between moments. This ancient principle guides everything we create.

Timeless Beauty in 10 Minutes

At 10min labo, we honor the Japanese philosophy of 'Ma' by creating products that work in harmony with your skin's natural rhythm, delivering transformative results in just ten minutes.

Each formula is a meditation on purity, precision, and the profound beauty of simplicity. We use only ingredients that have proven their worth over centuries, refined through modern science to unprecedented levels of efficacy.

Our Story

Born in Tokyo, Perfected Through Time

Founded in the heart of Ginza, Tokyo, 10min labo emerged from a simple yet revolutionary idea: that luxury skincare should not demand hours of your time, but should instead honor the precious moments of your day.

Our founder, a master formulator trained in both traditional Japanese beauty rituals and cutting-edge biotechnology, spent over a decade perfecting formulations that could deliver transformative results in just ten minutes.

Today, 10min labo represents the pinnacle of luxury skincare—where ancient wisdom meets modern science, where minimal time commitment yields maximum results, and where every product is a testament to the art of Ma.
