The name "10min labo" was born from a bold goal: skincare should feel baby-safe yet deliver professional results in minutes, not weeks. That pursuit led us to exosome technology, cell-penetrating water developed with Pro Labo Holdings, and transdermal delivery research that keeps every formula gentle enough for the entire family.

Today we combine high-concentration exosomes with TDDS and patented Innovative 3D Nanotechnology® to achieve rapid transformation while honoring our founding principle of family wellness over profit margins.

Our Story ストーリー

The Science of 10-Minute Transformation 10分の変化を支えるサイエンス

Our journey began with a challenge from families who wanted professional results without compromising safety. Years of research in regenerative medicine, exosome extraction, and TDDS technology made that vision real.

By collaborating with biotechnology institutes and Pro Labo Holdings, we perfected a surfactant-free delivery system where Innovative 3D Nanotechnology® escorts active ingredients deep into the skin through a simple massage ritual.

The result is proof that efficacy and baby-safe comfort can coexist—an award-winning formulation every member of the family can trust.

「安全性を損なうことなく、プロフェッショナルな結果を。」そんな家族からの声が、私たちの旅の始まりでした。 再生医療、エクソソーム抽出、TDDSテクノロジーに関する長年の研究が、そのビジョンを現実のものにしました。

バイオテクノロジー研究機関やPro Labo Holdingsとの協業により、界面活性剤に頼らずに有用成分を届ける デリバリーシステムを完成させ、革新的3Dナノテクノロジー®がシンプルなマッサージ習慣の中で成分を肌の奥へと エスコートします。
