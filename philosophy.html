Our Philosophy

At 10min labo, we embody this principle through relentless pursuit of purity and efficacy. Our singular commitment: formulate with ingredients so safe that even the most delicate baby skin can flourish.

This philosophy transcends business strategy—it defines who we are. While others optimize formulas for cost efficiency, we invest years in biotechnology research, rigorous safety protocols, and our revolutionary Innovative 3D Nanotechnology®. The result is not merely a product, but a promise: professional-grade transformation in 10 minutes, with absolute safety for every generation.

Our Story

Born from Research, Driven by Family

Our journey began with a question deemed impossible by industry experts: Could we create a cream gentle enough for a newborn's delicate skin, yet powerful enough to deliver professional anti-aging results? Most dismissed this as fantasy. We saw it as our calling.

Years of collaboration with Japan's premier biotechnology institutes led to our breakthrough—the world's first Innovative 3D Nanotechnology®. This patented delivery system revolutionizes how active ingredients penetrate skin barriers, achieving in 10 minutes what conventional creams require hours to accomplish.

From our atelier in Ginza's prestigious district, 10min labo has become living proof that luxury and safety are not opposing forces, but complementary virtues. Our Gold Award from "Professionals' Choice Cosmetics" validates what our families already knew: truly good products serve everyone, from the youngest to the wisest.

Our Values

Baby-Safe Materials

Our litmus test is uncompromising: would we apply this to a newborn? Every ingredient must earn its place through rigorous safety testing that far exceeds industry standards. If even the slightest doubt exists, it's excluded—no exceptions, no compromises.

Extensive Research

True innovation cannot be rushed. Our collaboration with Japan's leading biotechnology institutes spans years, not months. The result—our patented Innovative 3D Nanotechnology®—represents the culmination of countless iterations, failures, and breakthroughs in pursuit of perfection.

Family Wellness

Luxury skincare has traditionally been exclusive by design. We reject this paradigm. Our formulations embrace inclusivity—from the delicate epidermis of infants to mature skin seeking renewal. One exceptional cream, crafted for every generation, embodying wellness as a family value.

Rapid Results

Modern life demands efficiency without sacrificing quality. Through our revolutionary delivery system, professional-grade transformation occurs in just 10 minutes. Apply, massage, absorb—and witness your skin's innate radiance awakened through the marriage of science and artistry.
