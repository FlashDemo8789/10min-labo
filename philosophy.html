Our Story

The Science of 10-Minute Transformation

Our journey began with a bold challenge: create a cream that delivers visible results in 10 minutes. Traditional cosmetics require hours of application time, but we believed regenerative medicine technology could change this paradigm. Through extensive research in exosome science and transdermal delivery, we developed our breakthrough formulation.

The key innovation was combining multiple technologies: high-concentration exosomes from human stem cell culture liquid, cell-penetrating water from Pro Labo Holdings, TDDS (Transdermal Drug Delivery System), and our patented Innovative 3D Nanotechnology®. Together, these enable unprecedented penetration through the stratum corneum without surfactants.

The result: a surfactant-free cream with rich texture that feels surprisingly light and non-sticky. Through simple massage application, active ingredients gradually reach cells beneath the skin—delivering visible transformation in just 10 minutes. This is the promise that defines our name and our brand.
